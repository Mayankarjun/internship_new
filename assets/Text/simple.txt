### Advantages

1. **Cross-Platform Development**: Flutter allows you to write a single codebase for both iOS and Android, reducing development time and effort.
2. **Fast Development**: The hot reload feature lets you see changes in real-time without restarting the app, which speeds up development.
3. **Rich Widgets**: Flutter offers a wide range of pre-designed widgets and also allows you to create custom ones. This results in a highly customizable UI.
4. **Performance**: Flutter compiles to native code, which can lead to better performance compared to other cross-platform frameworks that use bridges or JavaScript.
5. **Unified Design**: With a single codebase, maintaining consistent design and behavior across platforms is easier.
6. **Growing Ecosystem**: A growing community and ecosystem provide a wealth of packages and plugins that enhance development efficiency.
7. **Google's Support**: Backed by Google, Flutter is continually updated and supported with new features and improvements.
